Privacy Policy – SWTCH E-Car Inc.

We at SWTCH E-Car Inc. (“we”, “us”, “our”, “SWTCH”) created this Privacy Policy ("Policy") because we know that you care about how information you provide to us is used and shared. We understand that privacy is important to our users, who use our website, including all subpages and successor pages (“Website”) and mobile applications, including iOS and Android applications (“App”).

SWTCH provides a marketplace application and website as well as associated media and support that helps its users ("Hosts") enter into agreements with other users ("Guests") to temporarily park and charge Guests' electric vehicles.

SWTCH will handle all Personal Information it receives in accordance with this Policy and the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), S.C. 2000, c.5.

1. What does this Privacy Policy apply to?

This Policy applies to the SWTCH Website and also applies to the SWTCH App.

This Policy does not apply to any website, product or service of any third-party company even if the website links to (or is linked from) our Website. SWTCH does not operate or control those websites, products or services. Please always review the privacy practices of a company before deciding whether to provide any information (Please see section 7. Use of Third Parties for more information).

By using our Website or App, you consent to and accept the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please delete all cookies from your internet browser cache after visiting our Website and do not visit or use our Website or App. Your continued use of our Website or App indicates your acceptance of this Policy.

2. Information collection and use

We collect information (1) when you provide it directly to us, (2) when we obtain verification information about you through trusted third parties (e.g. online payment platforms, banks), and (3) through packets of data that a website stores on your computer “cookies”. The types of information that we collect and our use of that information will depend on whether you are a Visitor, Guest, or Host, as described below. Our collection of this information helps SWTCH operate the Website and App and expand and market our services.

Personal Information

The term “Personal Information”, as used in this Policy, refers to information that can be used to identify a specific person, or any anonymous information (e.g., IP Address) that is linked to a specific person’s internet account. Personal information does not include aggregated anonymous information that can no longer be reasonably associated with a specific person.

You have a right to view your Personal Information.

We will keep your Personal Information accurate, complete and up-to-date with the information that you provide to us. If you request access to your Personal Information, we will inform you of the existence, use and disclosure of your Personal Information as allowed by law, and provide you access to that information. We will always ask for your consent before using your Personal Information for a purpose other than those described in this Privacy Policy.

Cookies

Similar to many commercial marketplaces, we use cookies and other technologies to collect non-personally-identifiable information from our Website. Cookies are a feature of web browser software that allows web servers to recognize the computer used to access a website. Information gathered through cookies may include information such as the date and time of visits, the pages viewed, IP addresses, links to/from any page, and time spent at a site. We use cookie data to measure web traffic and usage activity on our Website for purposes of improving and enhancing the functionality of our Website, to look for possible fraudulent activity, and to better understand the sources of traffic and transactions on our website and the websites of merchants that use our Website. You can choose to disable cookies for our Website but this may limit your ability to use our Website.

Web Server Logs

Web Server Log files are used to monitor, measure, analyze, improve, and troubleshoot our Website. The information may include the time and date of your visit to our website, pages viewed, links clicked and other non-personally identifying information.

Visitors

To simply browse our Website, you are not required to provide any Personal Information. However, we may gather non-personally-identifiable information, as described directly above, just for the purposes of monitoring and improving our Website. We will not share this information with third parties except as a necessary part of providing our Website and App, nor will we use it to target any advertisements to you.

Guests and Hosts

When you register for a SWTCH account, we collect Personal Information when you voluntarily provide it to us, including but not limited to your name, company name, location, email address, and phone number, and account password, to set up your account. We will also collect information on your IP addresses, devices, and locations used to access SWTCH, which will be linked to your account for fraud detection and prevention purposes. With your consent, we may also collect additional Personal Information in other ways including emails, surveys, and other forms of communication. Once you begin using the Service through your SWTCH account, we will keep records of your transactions and collect information of your other activities related to SWTCH.

Guests

When you use SWTCH as a Guest, we will gather and store your payment card information, email address, mobile phone number, billing and shipping address, license plate number and vehicle make, model and year. We will use this information to complete transactions that you choose to make on our Website and App, but only in accordance with our Terms of Service. We may also share your contact information, but not your card information, with merchants as part of your transaction. We will not share this information with other third parties except as a necessary part of providing services for our Website and App. You should carefully review third party services privacy policies in section 7 to better understand how each uses your information. (Please see section 7. Use of Third Parties for more information).

Hosts

When you access SWTCH as a Host, we will gather your email address, mobile phone number, billing and shipping address, bank account information, business and personal tax number, government-issued identification number and your date of birth, to verify your identity for fraud prevention purposes, provide payments to you in accordance with our payment policies, protect your account and to identify you if you contact support.

Location Data

When you use certain features of SWTCH, in particular the App, we may receive, store and process different types of information about your location, including general information (e.g. IP address, zip code) and more specific information (e.g. GPS-based functionality on mobile devices used to access the Website or App). If you access the Website or App through a mobile device and you do not want your device to provide us with location-tracking information, you can disable the GPS or other location-tracking functions on your device, provided your device allows you to do this, but this may limit your ability to use our Website or App. See your device manufacturer’s instructions for further details.

Device Information

We may collect information about your mobile device, including, for example, the hardware model, operating system and version, software and file names and versions, preferred language, unique device identifier, advertising identifiers, serial number, device motion information, and mobile network information.

Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act

Our Website and App are directed to the general public aged 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age or have any reasonable grounds for believing that children under the age of 13 are accessing our Website or using our App. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected Personal Information from a child under age 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

The use of your information by such third party will be subject to their applicable privacy policy, which you should carefully review before accessing the service.

In this event, we will notify you before your Personal Information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

If you elect not to provide Personal Information in optional fields it may limit your ability to use SWTCH. If you close your account, we will make best efforts to delete or anonymize all of your personal information that is not required to be kept under our data retention policy within a reasonable time.

Although no data transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to protect Personal Information and prevent its misuse, loss, or unauthorized access or disclosure. Other security safeguards include but are not limited to data encryption, IP whitelisting, firewalls, and physical access controls to building and files.
